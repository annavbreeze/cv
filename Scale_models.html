POLIGONAL PAVILION

FOR THE FIRST SCALED MODEL OF THE YEAR, BETWEEN GROUPS OF THREE WE WERE ASSIGNED THE TASK TO CREATE A SELF SUSTAINING PAVILION. THE MAIN DIFFICULTY OF THIS WOULD LIE IN THE FACT THAT IT HAD TO ACT AS A SHELTER, SO IT SHOULD BE DESIGNED WITH THE OBJECTIVE OF IT BEING A CLOSED STRUCTURE.

/* SECOND ROW */

ANOTHER REQUISITE WAS THAT IT COULD ONLY BE MADE OUT OF REGULAR POLYGONS. THE RECURRING DESIRE TO CREATE EXPERIMENTAL ARCHITECTURE,IS REFLECTED HERE WITH THE USE OF THE TRIANGLE NOT AS AN ELEMENT THAT INTERCONNECTS WITH ITSELF LIKE PATCHWORK, BUT RATHER THERE IS A FOCUS ON EXPLORING THE TEXTURES AND VOLUME THAT COULD ARISE BY LAYERING THE SAME SHAPE ONTO AND AROUND EACH MANY VERSIONS OF ITSELF

PAVILION DER LICHTER

THIS 1/20 SCALE MODEL HAS BEEN DESIGNED UNDER THE OBJECTIVE OF THE ASSIGNMENT TO CREATE A PAVILION WITH A SOLID FUNCTION THAT COULD POSSIBLY BE BROUGHT TO LIFE. THE OBJECTIVE CHOSEN IN THIS CASE WAS TO CREATE A READING SPACE, THE AMBIANCE OF WHICH HAS PLACED AN IMPORTANT FACTOR IN THE DESIGN PROCESS, THE REASON WHY DIM LIGHT SEEPS IN THROUGH THE INTERSTITIAL SPACES BETWEEN THE NUMEROUS UNDULATING CARDBOARD SHEETS.

/* FIFTH ROW */

THE STRUCTURE ALSO PROVIDES A BOOKSHELF AND HAS ALLOTTED SECRET READING SPACES LOCATED TO THE LEFT AND RIGHT OF THE INTERIOR'S FAR END, PROVIDING PRIVACY TO THE READERS WHO DELVE THIS DEEP INTO IT

2020

LONDON ZOO PENGUIN SWIMMING POOL

IN A PERIOD OF THREE HOURS WE WERE ASKED TO STUDY THE PENGUIN SWIMMING POOL IN LONDON ZOO BY LUBETKIN AND PROCEED TO MAKE A 1/100 SCALED MODEL OF IT. THE ONE IN HAND UNDERLINES ITS HIGHLIGHTS, WHICH ARE CONSIDERED TO BE THE POOL'S INTERESTINGLY SHAPED RAMPS AND THE PERSPECTIVE THEY OFFER TO THE VISITOR PASSING BY
